We love Bali's
coffee scene.

Taplo started because we were tired of losing paper stamp cards. Every cafe had a different one, they'd get soggy in the rain, forgotten at the villa, or just lost. We thought: what if your loyalty card lived in your phone, worked everywhere, and never got lost?

The Mission

Help every independent cafe build real customer loyalty.

Big chains have apps, data, and loyalty programs. Your local cafe has great coffee and a paper card. We're here to level that playing field.

Taplo gives independent cafes the same loyalty tools as Starbucks — without the app, the complexity, or the cost.

Bali's cafe culture is unique. It's a mix of locals, expats, digital nomads, and tourists — all passionate about their daily coffee. But most cafes still rely on paper stamp cards or nothing at all. That's the gap Taplo fills.

What we believe

Simplicity wins

No app to download. No account to create. If it takes more than 3 seconds, we've failed.

Local first

We build for Bali's cafes, not Silicon Valley's metrics. What works here matters more than what scales there.

Privacy by design

We collect the minimum data needed. Your coffee habits are yours. We never sell data to third parties.

Earn trust, then revenue

We're free during launch because we want to prove value before asking for anything. That's the Bali way.
