Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is my personal data safe with Taplo? expand_more
We use encryption and never sell your data to third parties. Your privacy is our priority. We only share your visit data with the specific cafes you choose to support, so they can give you a more personal experience.
Can I use my stamps at multiple cafes? expand_more
Yes! Your Taplo pass works across all partner cafes. Earn stamps at any participating location in Canggu — one pass covers all of them.
How do I become a partner merchant? expand_more
Head to our For Cafes page and get in touch via WhatsApp or email. Setup takes about 15 minutes and it's free during our launch period.
Do I need to download an app? expand_more
Nope! Taplo works entirely through Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. Just scan the QR code at the counter and your pass saves instantly. No app, no account, no sign-up form.
How many stamps do I need for a free coffee? expand_more
Each cafe sets their own reward rules — typically 5 stamps gets you a free coffee. Your pass shows your current progress in real time, and you'll get a lock screen notification when you're close.
